FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How can I book a ride? To book a ride, simply visit our website and click on the 'Book a Ride' button. Follow the instructions to select your pickup and drop-off locations, date, and time.
What types of vehicles are available? We offer a variety of vehicles suitable for senior citizens, including sedans, wheelchair-accessible vans, and larger vehicles for group transportation.
Are the drivers trained to assist senior citizens? Yes, all our drivers are trained to provide assistance to senior citizens. They are experienced in handling mobility aids and ensuring a comfortable and safe journey.
Can I schedule recurring rides? Yes, you can schedule recurring rides for regular appointments or activities. Simply provide the details of your recurring schedule during the booking process.
What if I need to cancel or modify my ride? If you need to cancel or modify your ride, please contact our customer support team at least 24 hours in advance. They will assist you in making the necessary changes.
